# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior: empathy, respect for differing viewpoints, constructive feedback, accountability.

Unacceptable behavior: harassment, trolling, publishing private information, unprofessional conduct.
